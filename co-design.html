Co-Design

What is co-design?

Co-design is a collaborative process involving stakeholders and end-users throughout the entire development process of the project to ensure that the end outcomes meet the needs of key stakeholders and provide a system which is useful, usable and valuable to your work with micronutrients

This began with a number of in-person workshops in multiple nations and will continue as the project develops both in-person and remotely e.g. via internet videoconferencing.

Co-design is a an approach to design attempting to actively involve all stakeholders (e.g. employees, partners, customers, citizens, end users) in the design process to help ensure the end result meets their needs and is usable
